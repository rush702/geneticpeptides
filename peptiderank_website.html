The Process
How PeptideRank Works

Our AI engine runs daily to give you the most current picture of every vendor in the market.

1

Daily Data Crawl

Automated crawlers scrape all 350+ indexed vendor websites, pulling product pages, COA documents, pricing, and policies every 24 hours.

2

COA AI Verification

Our OCR + GPT model reads every Certificate of Analysis, extracts purity percentages, validates HPLC/MS data, and flags inconsistencies.

3

Reddit & Community Scan

We pull posts from r/Peptides, r/nootropics, forums, and Discord every 6 hours, running sentiment classification on vendor mentions.

4

Score Computation

All five data pillars are weighted and combined into the PeptideRank Score™ — recalculated daily with anomaly detection alerts.

5

Publish & Notify

Rankings update live on the platform. Vendors with significant score changes are notified via their dashboard within hours.
