The Process
How PeptideVerify Works

Five data sources. One transparent score. Updated automatically every 24 hours.

1

Daily Site Crawl

Automated crawlers scrape all 350+ vendor websites — product pages, COA documents, pricing — every 24 hours with polite rate limits.

2

COA AI Parsing

OCR + LLM reads every Certificate of Analysis, extracts purity %, validates HPLC/MS data, cross-checks batch numbers vs. product labels.

3

Reddit Intelligence

r/Peptides, r/nootropics, r/Biohackers polled every 6 hours. BERT sentiment model scores vendor mentions in context — not just keyword counts.

4

Finnrick Integration

6,102+ independent lab test results from Finnrick Analytics are pulled and weighted into purity scores for all covered vendors.

5

Score & Publish

All five pillars are weighted and combined into the PeptideVerify Score™, recalculated daily with anomaly alerts for significant drops.
