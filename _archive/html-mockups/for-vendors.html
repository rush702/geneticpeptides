Scoring Methodology

How Your Score is Calculated

Our AI aggregates five independent data pillars every 24 hours. Scores are algorithmic — no human can buy a better rank.

🧪
COA Purity Score
We fetch, parse, and validate certificates of analysis. HPLC ≥ 98%, MS confirmation, and vendor-signed docs all factor in. Unverifiable COAs score zero.
Weight: 30%
🌐
Reddit & Forum Sentiment
NLP analysis of r/Peptides, r/PeptidesResearch, Eroids, and other community forums. Recency-weighted so a bad month can't permanently tank a reformed vendor.
Weight: 25%
⭐
Verified Customer Reviews
Cryptographically linked reviews from confirmed purchasers. Bot-resistant. Covers delivery speed, product quality, packaging, and communication.
Weight: 20%
🎧
Customer Service
Average first-response time, resolution rate, and refund/reship handling gathered from community reports and our own undercover inquiry testing.
Weight: 15%
💰
Price Competitiveness
Price-per-mg benchmarked daily across the market. Transparency of pricing pages, bulk discounts, and hidden-fee penalties are all included.
Weight: 10%

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I be removed from the rankings if I don't pay?

No. All publicly operating peptide vendors are indexed automatically and remain in the rankings regardless of plan. Our independence depends on it. Paying for a Pro or Enterprise plan gives you tools to understand and improve your score — it does not affect your rank algorithm.

Can I pay to boost my score or rank?

Absolutely not. Scores are purely algorithmic — derived from COA analysis, community sentiment, verified reviews, customer service data, and pricing. No payment, partnership, or advertising arrangement influences your composite score or rank position. This is non-negotiable and core to our value proposition.

How do I dispute an incorrect score or data point?

Claimed vendors can submit a data dispute through their dashboard. Provide evidence (e.g., updated COA, screenshot correcting a misattributed Reddit post) and our team reviews within 5 business days. Valid disputes trigger a manual score recalculation. All disputes and outcomes are logged for transparency.

What COA formats do you accept?

We accept PDF, PNG, and JPG formats. COAs must include: peptide name, lot number, HPLC purity percentage, mass spectrometry confirmation, and issuing lab name. COAs from third-party ISO-accredited labs score higher than in-house documents. We do not accept COAs without spectrometry data.

How long does Verified Partner status take?

Typically 24–48 hours after you submit your COAs and complete onboarding. Fast-track partners (Verified plan) have a guaranteed 24-hour SLA for COA review. Badge embedding code is available immediately upon approval.

Do you score international vendors?

Yes. We currently index vendors from the US, EU, Canada, Australia, and China. Scoring methodology is applied consistently regardless of geography. Some community data sources (e.g., specific Reddit communities) may have less volume for non-English-language vendors — this is reflected transparently in score confidence intervals.
